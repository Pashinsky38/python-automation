1. General Information About Koalas

Koalas are marsupials native to Australia, known for their distinctive appearance and tree-dwelling habits.
They have a stout, tailless body and large, fluffy ears. Koalas primarily feed on eucalyptus leaves, which provide them with most of their water needs.
They are mostly nocturnal and spend a significant amount of time sleeping, often up to 18-22 hours a day. Koalas are an iconic symbol of Australian wildlife and are protected by law due to habitat loss and other environmental threats.
